Meet Sam Bogen

Founder & Lead Organizer

With over 10 years of experience in luxury home organization, Sam Bogen brings passion, creativity, and meticulous attention to detail to every project. Her approach combines the art of interior design with practical organizing solutions to create spaces that are both beautiful and functional.

Based in Palm Beach, Florida, Sam specializes in helping busy families reclaim their homes and simplify their lives. Whether you're overwhelmed by clutter, preparing for a move, or simply want to optimize your living spaces, Sam and her team are here to guide you through the transformation.
